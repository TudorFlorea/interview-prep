## ðŸŽ¯ Why Behavioral Interviews Matter

Behavioral interviews assess:
- **Culture Fit**: How you align with company values
- **Past Performance**: Past behavior predicts future behavior
- **Soft Skills**: Communication, leadership, conflict resolution
- **Self-Awareness**: How you learn from failures and challenges

Most companies allocate **30-50%** of interview time to behavioral rounds.

## ðŸ“‹ Quick Reference

### STAR Method Template
- **Situation**: Set context (1-2 sentences)
- **Task**: Your responsibility (1 sentence)
- **Action**: What YOU did (3-4 sentences)
- **Result**: Quantifiable impact (1-2 sentences)

## ðŸŽ“ Study Tips

1. **Quality over Quantity**: 10 well-prepared stories > 20 shallow ones
2. **Quantify Results**: Always include metrics (%, $, time saved)
3. **Show Growth**: Discuss what you learned from failures
4. **Be Specific**: Use "I" not "we" to show your personal contribution
5. **Stay Recent**: Prefer stories from last 2-3 years
6. **Practice Out Loud**: Saying answers feels different than thinking them
7. **Keep it Concise**: Aim for 2-3 minutes per answer
8. **Cross-Reference**: Link behavioral stories to technical knowledge
