## üëë Leadership & Influence

### Q1: Tell me about a time you led a project or initiative

**What They're Looking For**:
- Taking initiative and ownership
- Influencing without authority
- Achieving results through others

**STAR Approach**:
- **Situation**: Describe the project and why leadership was needed
- **Task**: Your specific leadership role
- **Action**: How you organized, motivated, and guided the team; decisions you made
- **Result**: Project outcomes and team development

**Red Flags**:
- ‚ùå Only talking about what YOU did (not leading others)
- ‚ùå Micromanaging instead of empowering
- ‚ùå Taking all credit without acknowledging team

**Related Stories**: Use leadership stories from your [Story Bank](02-story-bank.md)

**Amazon LP**: Ownership, Deliver Results  
**Google**: Leadership, Collaboration  
**Microsoft**: One Microsoft
